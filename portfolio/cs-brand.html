01 — Brief
A new product, no visual language

The founders had a clear product vision but nothing visual. Their pitch deck used stock photos and system fonts. They needed a brand that could stand up in investor decks, a product launch, and social media — all at once.

The challenge: create something that feels premium and trustworthy, but not corporate. Ambitious but accessible. Built to scale as the company grows.

02 — Process
Discovery → Strategy → Design → Refine
1
Brand discovery workshop
3-hour session with the founders covering mission, values, audience, competitors, and personality. Generated 40+ positioning statements, narrowed to 3.
2
Moodboards & visual territory
3 distinct visual directions presented: Modern Minimal, Bold & Playful, and Premium Dark. Client selected direction 2 with elements from direction 3.
3
Logo exploration
20 logo sketches → 6 refined concepts → 2 final options. Selected mark is a geometric monogram that works at 16px favicon and 10m billboard.
4
System build & guidelines
Colour system, type scale, spacing, icon style, photography direction, tone of voice. Everything documented in a 40-page brand guide.
03 — Visual Language
Warm, bold, purposeful

The palette pairs a warm amber primary with a coral accent — energetic but not aggressive. Dark navy grounds the system for premium touchpoints. Every colour has a defined role.
