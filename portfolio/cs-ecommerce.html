01 — Problem
A leaky funnel losing customers at every step

Analytics showed an 82% drop-off between cart and payment. Exit surveys pointed to a confusing checkout — too many form fields, unclear shipping costs, and a forced account creation before purchase.

Mobile accounted for 68% of traffic but only 31% of conversions. The experience was clearly built desktop-first and never properly adapted.

📉
Before — 7-step checkout with 3 mandatory account screens
02 — Process
Data-led, user-validated
1
Funnel analysis & heatmaps
Analysed 90 days of session recordings and funnel data. Pinpointed exact drop-off points and rage-click hotspots.
2
Competitive benchmarking
Audited checkout flows of 12 top-performing e-commerce sites. Identified patterns that reduce friction at payment.
3
Mobile-first redesign
Rebuilt every screen starting at 375px. Guest checkout, inline validation, and persistent order summary panel.
4
A/B test → full rollout
Ran a 2-week A/B test with 50/50 traffic split. Results confirmed; rolled out to 100% of users.
03 — Solution
3 steps, no surprises, no friction

The new checkout is a single progressive form — shipping, payment, confirm. Costs are shown upfront. Guest checkout is the default. On mobile, the CTA is always visible above the fold.
